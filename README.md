## Executive Summary

**LearnWhitehouse** is a specialized educational technology platform designed to address the critical challenge of high failure rates in chemistry education at our institution's Whitehouse Department. By leveraging advanced AI technology and department-specific course materials, we provide personalized, intelligent tutoring that adapts to each student's learning needs and academic level.

### The Problem
- **High Failure Rates**: 40-60% of chemistry students in Whitehouse Department fail their courses annually
- **Limited Support**: Traditional lectures alone are insufficient for diverse learning styles
- **Generic AI Limitations**: Standard AI models lack deep knowledge of Whitehouse Department's specific chemistry curricula
- **One-Size-Fits-All**: Current solutions don't adapt to individual student needs or academic levels

### Our Solution
A specialized AI tutoring system that learns from Whitehouse Department's actual course materials, providing personalized, context-aware assistance that grows with each student's progress through advanced RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) technology.
